## â“ Common Questions

### Q: How many decals can I add?
**A:** No hard limit, but 10-20 decals is recommended for performance.

### Q: Can I save my decals with my vehicle?
**A:** Yes! Decals are saved with your vehicle configuration.

### Q: What if the decal is in the wrong position?
**A:** Click on it in the list to select it, then click a new spot on the vehicle.

### Q: Can I rotate decals?
**A:** Yes! Select the decal and use the Rotation slider in the editor.

### Q: Can I make decals transparent?
**A:** Yes! Use the Opacity slider to adjust transparency.

### Q: My decal is too big/small!
**A:** Select it and use the Size slider to adjust.

### Q: How do I delete a decal?
**A:** Click the trash icon (ğŸ—‘ï¸) next to it in the list.

### Q: Can I upload my own logo?
**A:** Absolutely! Just click "Upload Decal" and select your logo file.

## ğŸ› Troubleshooting

### Problem: "Decal won't upload"
**Solutions:**
- Check file format (PNG, JPG, SVG, WebP only)
- Check file size (must be under 5MB)
- Try a different image

### Problem: "Can't see the decal after placing"
**Solutions:**
- Check opacity (might be set to 0%)
- Check scale (might be too small)
- Try clicking a different area of the vehicle
- Look at different angles of the vehicle

### Problem: "Decal is in the wrong spot"
**Solutions:**
- Select the decal in the list
- Click on the vehicle where you want to move it
- The decal will jump to the new position

### Problem: "Decal looks blurry"
**Solutions:**
- Use a higher resolution image
- Reduce the scale (smaller decals look sharper)
- Use PNG format instead of JPG
